Ordering

Browse our shop, select your desired product, choose the quantity and click "Add to Cart". Once you're ready, go to your cart and proceed to checkout. Fill in your billing details and click "Place Order".

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 24 hours of placement. Please contact our support team immediately at info@djangomart.com with your order details.

Shipping & Delivery

Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days. Express delivery (1-2 business days) is available for an additional fee. International orders may take 7-14 business days.

Yes! We offer free standard shipping on all orders. No minimum order value required.

Yes, once your order is shipped you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this to track your delivery in real time on our website.
Returns & Refunds

We offer a 14-day hassle-free return policy. Items must be unused, in original packaging, and in the same condition you received them. Simply contact us to initiate a return.

Once we receive your returned item, refunds are processed within 3-5 business days. The amount will be credited back to your original payment method.
